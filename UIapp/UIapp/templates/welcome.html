    Services

    Track accounts

    Setup a new project, choose Facebook pages, Twitter accounts and hashtags/keywords you want to track and Alnzer will collect these open data. Have you changed your mind about the content to track? Change your project settings and see new results coming.

    Run sentiment analysis

    On the collected data, Anlzer runs sentiment analysis to bring out new trends and user opinions. The Anlzer can be re-trained to fit your industry needs.

    See live results

    Want to identify trends? You can go through free search and see relative trends.

    Export reports

    Create queries on the collected data, see diagrams and export reports to facilitate your decisions.

    Meet the team

    Want to know more? | a little about us

    We are a team of researchers working on fancy semantic and sentiment technologies over social media. We do our research at DSSLab (Decision Support Systems laboratory) laboratory, in NTUA (National Technical University of Athens), while we work to ground our knowledge on handy tools to run our data analyses.

    Our goal is to create easy to use and useful tools to bring data technologies closer to industrial solutions. We love to share our work and get feedback, so do not hesitate to contact us in case you have any question or comment.
